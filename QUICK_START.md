## Tips & Tricks

### 1. Get Better AI Responses
- **Be specific**: "Engineering colleges in Srinagar" vs "colleges"
- **Ask follow-ups**: "Tell me about the admission process"
- **Use examples**: "I'm interested in coding and science"

### 2. Maximize Assessment
- **Answer honestly**: No "right" answers, just right for you
- **Take your time**: Think about each question
- **Trust yourself**: Your gut feeling is often correct
- **Review results**: Understand what they mean for you

### 3. Create Better Roadmap
- **Be realistic**: Choose achievable goals
- **Set milestones**: Break big goals into steps
- **Review regularly**: Update as you progress
- **Stay flexible**: Adjust based on interests

### 4. Navigate Efficiently
- **Use quick actions**: Save time with button shortcuts
- **Bookmark important info**: Save answers for later
- **Check notifications**: Don't miss deadlines
- **Customize settings**: Personalize your experience
